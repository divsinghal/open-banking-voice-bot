Azure4Alexa


It's easy to build and host your Alexa Custom Skill with Visual Studio and Azure!

You can even access Oauth 2.0 protected services such as Microsoft Outlook. Follow the instructions in Outlook/Mail.cs to link your Alexa Custom Skill to your upgraded Outlook.com personal account, or in most cases, your work and school Outlook account.
